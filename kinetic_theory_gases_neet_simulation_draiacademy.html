Kinetic theory connects molecular motion with gas laws and thermodynamics.

NEET key: RMS speed depends on temperature and molar mass, not directly on pressure.

Simple NEET Explanation

  • Gas molecules move randomly in all directions.
  • Pressure is produced when molecules collide with the container walls.
  • Higher temperature means faster molecular motion.
  • RMS speed is the root mean square speed of molecules.
  • Lighter gases have higher RMS speed at the same temperature.
  • Internal energy of ideal gas depends only on temperature.
  • Degrees of freedom tell how many independent ways a molecule can store energy.

NEET Traps

  • Temperature is related to average kinetic energy, not potential energy.
  • RMS speed is not same as average speed.
  • Use molar mass in kg/mol in vrms formula.
  • Pressure increases when volume decreases at constant temperature.
  • Internal energy of ideal gas depends only on temperature.
  • Monoatomic gas has 3 degrees of freedom.
  • Diatomic gas usually has 5 degrees of freedom at room temperature.
  • PV = nRT requires volume in m³ and pressure in Pa.
